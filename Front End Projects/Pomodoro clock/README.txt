The Pomodoro Clock is a well-known productivity interval that has been shown to improve your productivity. It gives you a prescribed interval of 25 minutes of work followed by a 5-minute break. After 4 work intervals, there is a 15-minute break. If you want to get started on a Pomodoro Timer, just click the Pomodoro button above.

Developed using Vanilla Javascript & LESS.

You can find this one at http://codepen.io/gandhi_rahul/pen/WoENjN.
